Frequently Asked Questions

What counts as a "qualified lead"?▼

A qualified lead is a real person or family member actively searching for care services in your area. We verify every inquiry before sending it to your facility â no bots, no duplicates, no tire-kickers.

How are leads delivered?▼

Leads are delivered instantly via email and SMS to the contact information on your vendor profile. You'll also see all leads in your Vendor Portal dashboard with full contact details.

Can I cancel anytime?▼

Absolutely. There are no contracts or commitments. You can cancel your subscription at any time from your Vendor Portal or by contacting our support team. Your plan stays active until the end of the billing period.

What if I run out of leads for the month?▼

If you reach your monthly lead limit, you can upgrade your plan at any time. The price difference is prorated. Premium plan members receive unlimited leads so you'll never hit a cap.

What payment methods do you accept?▼

We accept all major credit and debit cards (Visa, Mastercard, American Express, Discover). Payments are processed securely through Stripe.
