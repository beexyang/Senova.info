About

Sunshine Home Health Services has been providing compassionate home health care to the Houston community since 2008. We specialize in Medicaid-covered personal care, skilled nursing, and therapy services. Our team of licensed nurses and certified home health aides are dedicated to helping seniors and individuals with disabilities maintain their independence at home.
