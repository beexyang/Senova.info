Our Mission

We exist so families don't have to figure this out alone.

Finding good care for someone you love is one of the hardest searches a person ever does. Senova exists to make that search short, honest, and free — and to put real, government-listed providers in front of the families who need them.

Why Senova

Most people only search for care once or twice in their life — and they're scared, exhausted, and short on time.

Maybe a parent just had a stroke. A spouse needs hospice. A teenager needs treatment. Whatever brought you here, you're probably tired of click-bait directories that bury providers behind paywalls, charge them to be listed, and feed you sales calls instead of answers. That's not what Senova is. Every facility in our directory comes from a federal source — CMS for home health and hospice, SAMHSA for substance use and mental health treatment — refreshed automatically. We don't sell rankings. We don't gate listings. We don't take payment to list a provider higher than another.

For families

Search by ZIP, filter by Medicaid, level of care, languages spoken, populations served — and call the providers directly. No middleman, no callback funnel, no pressure. Every provider's listing is tied to the exact data their state and federal regulators publish about them.

For providers

If you're a CMS-listed home health or hospice agency, or a SAMHSA-listed treatment facility, you're already in our directory — for free. No ads, no pay-to-rank. Sign up for a provider account to manage your incoming leads and track who reached out.

What we believe

Three principles, every page on this site.

1. Source-of-truth data

We list providers from official federal rosters (CMS, SAMHSA) and state agencies. If a federal source doesn't say a facility offers a service, we don't claim it does.

2. No pay-to-play

We do not take money to rank a provider higher, hide a competitor, or fabricate "verified" badges. Trust is the only thing healthcare directories have to offer — we're not selling it.

3. Honest about limits

We tell you when source data is missing, when a state's site is down, and when a question is better answered by your state's program directly. Senova doesn't pretend to be the whole healthcare system — we just point you at the right part of it.
