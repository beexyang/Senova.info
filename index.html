Home Health Care Directory — All 50 States

Senova is a free home health care directory covering all 50 US states. Find Medicaid-covered home health agencies, skilled nursing services, home health aides, personal care attendants, and physical therapy services near you. Our directory includes providers that accept Medicaid Title 19, HCBS waivers (Section 1915c), Medicare Part A, and private insurance.

Adult Day Care Programs Directory

Search adult day care programs and adult day health centers in every state. Senova lists Medicaid-covered adult day programs including social day programs, medical adult day care, memory care day programs, and respite care for caregivers. Many adult day programs are covered by Medicaid HCBS waivers at no cost to families.

Hospice Care Directory

Find hospice care providers near you. Senova's hospice directory includes in-home hospice, inpatient hospice facilities, palliative care centers, and bereavement support services. Hospice care is covered 100% by Medicare Part A and Medicaid in most states.

Assisted Living Directory

Search assisted living facilities, residential care homes, adult foster care, and memory care communities. Many states offer Medicaid assisted living waivers that pay for assisted living at low or no cost to residents who qualify.

Drug and Alcohol Rehabilitation Directory

Find Medicaid-covered drug rehab and alcohol rehabilitation programs with housing. Senova lists residential inpatient rehab, sober living homes that accept Medicaid, intensive outpatient programs, and transitional housing for recovery. Most states cover substance use disorder (SUD) treatment through Medicaid waivers.

PACE Programs Directory

The Program of All-Inclusive Care for the Elderly (PACE) provides comprehensive medical and social care covered 100% by Medicare and Medicaid. PACE programs are available in 32+ states. Search PACE program locations near you on Senova.

Medicaid Title 19 and HCBS Waiver Guide

Every state offers Medicaid Home and Community-Based Services (HCBS) waivers under Section 1915(c) of the Social Security Act. These waivers allow Medicaid to pay for care services in the home or community including home health aides, personal care, adult day care, respite care, and assisted living. Senova helps families understand what Medicaid programs are available in their state and which providers accept them.
